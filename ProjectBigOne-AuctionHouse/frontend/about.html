About Us

About Us

Welcome to LuxeAuto Auctions, the ultimate destination where luxury meets performance in the world of automotive excellence. Founded in 2024 by Dawid Maciejewski, LuxeAuto stands as a premier hub for enthusiasts seeking the epitome of luxury vehicles.
Our mission at LuxeAuto is simple: to provide a curated selection of the finest luxury automobiles available. From rare classics to cutting-edge supercars, every vehicle in our inventory undergoes rigorous inspection to ensure authenticity and superior quality.
What sets LuxeAuto apart is our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Our team of experts is dedicated to assisting clients throughout the purchasing process, offering personalized services tailored to their unique needs.
At LuxeAuto Auctions, we invite you to explore the unparalleled world of automotive luxury. Join us on our journey and discover your dream car today.

Who We Are

LuxeAuto Auctions is your gateway to the most coveted luxury automobiles on the market. Our team of dedicated experts is committed to providing an unparalleled auction experience, connecting buyers and sellers worldwide with ease.

History

Established by Dawid Maciejewski, LuxeAuto Auctions emerged from a passion for luxury automobiles and a desire to create a dynamic platform for enthusiasts. Since our inception, we've redefined the luxury automotive auction landscape, setting new standards of excellence in service and selection.
